The Beginning

Born From A Passion
For Perfection.

Vasave Cafe wasn't just built; it was curated. Founded in 2015 by two childhood friends, Rahul and Arjun, who spent years backpacking through the coffee plantations of Coorg and Chikmagalur.

They realized that despite India being a major coffee producer, the local cafe culture was missing the authentic, farm-to-cup connection. They set out to change that.

What started as a small 400 sq. ft. outlet in Indiranagar has now grown into a sanctuary for coffee lovers, digital nomads, and artists.
